Residential Glass Repair in Baltimore

Homeowners across Federal Hill, Charles Village, Mount Vernon, and Parkville call us when a window won’t close safely, a patio door panel cracks, or a shower door fails. We’re a commercial-first team by volume—but residential glass repair gets the same straight answers on what’s fixable tonight vs. what needs ordering.

Home window glass repair

Cracked panes, failed seals (“foggy” glass), and storm-season damage are common. We walk through repair vs. full replacement on window glass repair.

Patio door glass

Sliding and swinging patio units see impact and track wear. Sometimes the glass is the issue; sometimes alignment is. We’ll tell you which we’re seeing.

Shower doors & mirrors

Bath upgrades and rental turnovers often need shower door work and mirror replacement—especially in rowhomes where baths are tight and hardware tolerances matter.

Emergency home glass

If an opening is unsecured, board up may apply to homes as well—particularly ground-floor bedrooms or rear doors in Dundalk and Essex waterfront-adjacent blocks with weather exposure.
