How Long Does Commercial Glass Replacement Usually Take?

Commercial · Timelines

Honest answer: it depends. A standard door lite in a common size may be same-day; a oversized storefront vision panel may require fabrication. Here’s how we estimate in Baltimore jobs without overpromising.

Stock vs custom

Supplier inventory and tempering schedules matter. Night calls can miss same-day supplier runs—board up bridges that gap.

Hardware and access

Closer adjustments, panic devices, and wind loads aren’t “glass-only” problems. If the door needs work beyond the lite, we flag it early.

Weather

Sealants and install conditions for exterior work can shift schedules—especially in shoulder seasons along waterfront-adjacent sites.
