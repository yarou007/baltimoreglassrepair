Can You Tape or Seal Broken Glass Safely for One Night?

Tape can mark visibility for people walking toward a broken lite—it does not recreate structural integrity. For compromised tempered glass, assume sudden collapse is possible; tape won’t hold weight or forced entry.

When plastic sheeting helps

Interior storms: a taped poly sheet can reduce drafts if shards are stable and you’re not leaning on the sash. It’s not a security layer.

When to call for board up

Ground-floor commercial openings, missing lites, or weather exposure—see emergency board up and broken window safety.
