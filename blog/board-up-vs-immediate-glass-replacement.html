Board Up vs Immediate Glass Replacement: Which First?

April 2026 · Emergency board up · Glass replacement

The question sounds binary—board up or replace—but the answer is usually sequential. In Baltimore, weather exposure, security risk, and whether a custom lite is in stock determine whether we can complete replacement immediately or secure first and return.

When board up comes first

  • Custom storefront sizes not available off the shelf
  • Storm conditions or overnight risk where an open hole is unacceptable
  • Scenes requiring stabilization before precise measurements
  • Insurance or security asks for temporary secure before permanent work

We describe our board up service as a bridge—not a substitute for proper glazing.

When replacement can happen immediately

  • Standard door lites we stock or can source same-day from suppliers
  • Residential window glass with common dimensions
  • Scenarios where the frame is sound and the measurement is straightforward

Commercial operations angle

Property teams in downtown Baltimore and Canton often need a written sequence for tenants: “secure tonight, install Thursday.” We help you speak precisely—see property managers guide.
