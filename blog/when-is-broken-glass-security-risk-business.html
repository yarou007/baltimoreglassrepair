When Is Broken Glass a Security Risk for Your Business?

Security isn’t only about break-ins—it’s about predictability. If patrons can’t tell where the hazard begins, you’ve crossed a threshold. Indicators:

  • Missing lites at street level after hours
  • Door glass that compromises locking or latch engagement
  • Sharp debris in a travel path
