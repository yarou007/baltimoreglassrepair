Do You Need Tempered or Laminated Glass for Your Business?

Tempered glass is heat-treated for strength; when it fails, it tends to break into small pieces—often why entire door lites get replaced after meaningful cracks. Laminated glass sandwiches a plastic interlayer—helpful where retention matters and for certain security goals. Code and application drive the choice—not marketing.
