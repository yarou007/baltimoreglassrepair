Common situations we solve

Broken storefront after a break-in

You need police clearance (when applicable), a secure perimeter, and a path for customers or tenants. We coordinate temporary protection first—see emergency board up in Baltimore—then glass replacement.

Office entry door glass cracked

Tempered glass often fails completely once compromised; we’ll advise whether the door can stay in limited use or should be cordoned until repair.

Home window cracked in cold weather
