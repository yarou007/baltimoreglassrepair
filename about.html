About Baltimore Glass Repair

We’re a Baltimore-focused glass team built around emergencies: the call that comes after a break-in on a Fells Point block, the restaurant door that won’t secure on a Friday night, the home window that fails in a cold snap. Our highest volume is commercial glass repair—because when storefronts fail, the clock is loud. We also provide full residential glass repair for homeowners who need clarity, not jargon.

What we believe

  • Secure first. If an opening isn’t safe or weather-tight, temporary protection can be the right move.
  • Explain tradeoffs. Repair vs. replacement depends on glass type, damage, and code— we spell that out.
  • Stay local in language. We reference real corridors—Inner Harbor, Canton, Dundalk, Towson—because search intent and lived experience match.
